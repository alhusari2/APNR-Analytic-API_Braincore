How to Use:

  • Click the "Start Detection" button to begin.
  • Upload an image containing vehicle license plates.
  • Wait for the system to process and display the recognized license plates.
  • Download or review the results as needed.

This system is designed for efficient and accurate recognition of license plates in various formats. For best results, ensure the images or videos are clear and the plates are fully visible.
